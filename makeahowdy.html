Making your Howdy

Making a Howdy is simple!

If you find someone cool just tell them that when they are free (if they are) you'd love to meet for a coffee or for a music, cooking, coding (etc... etc...) session!

You can also download the PDF and print it out, and just hand it to people randomly as you see them, there should be a black box for you to write your details and where to meet.
